About me

I work as a web designer for a technology company. I started working in web design a little over 5 years ago, but my interest in it dates back to 2013 when I began training in graphic, web, and interactive design.

In my experience, I have been involved in a variety of projects, such as:

  • App design
  • Web design and layout
  • 3D design and programming for video games
  • Graphic design for digital and print formats
